Basic Theory & Strategy

Equity

Equity is the odds that your hand will win after all cards are dealt in a vacuum against your opponents hand/range. Important: A hand with more equity than another hand does not make it a more profitable hand.

Optimal Bluffing Frequency

0.5PSB (Pot sized bet) bluff = 33% Win rate required to break-even
--> villain required to call 66% to remain balanced.
(.66%)(0.5PSB) = 0.33PSB on average committed

Editor comments

Defend around 30% of opens to 3bets by 4betting.
